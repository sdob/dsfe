divesit.es is

a web application for finding places to go diving. You look at the map and click on things, and then you find out about them.

This is a divesite, where people go diving. This is usually done in water, but sometimes plans go awry.

This is a slipway, where people launch boats. The fat end goes in first, just like in the picture. You see? It's helpful!

This is a compressor, where people fill tanks. That's about all there is to say about compressors.
